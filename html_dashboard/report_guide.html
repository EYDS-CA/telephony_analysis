Key Takeaway

Our analysis of 12,785 app reviews reveals a fundamental truth: success in telecom apps isn't about beautiful UI—it's about bulletproof core functions. The data shows both Rogers and Bell score identically (2.64/5) despite Bell's superior design, proving that the winning formula is to build a "banking app that pays telecom bills" with absolute reliability for authentication, payments, and technical stability.
