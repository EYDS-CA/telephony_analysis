Key Insights & Recommendations

🎯 Primary Finding

Rogers app has nearly 2x more app-related complaints (39.2%) compared to Bell (20.8%), while Bell users complain more about service issues. This indicates significant room for app improvement.

📱 Platform Disparity

Android users experience 4x more crashes than iOS users (3.4% vs 0.8%). Android app stability should be prioritized.

💰 Business Impact

Fixing the top 3 app issues could reduce customer service contact by 30-40% and improve app ratings by up to 1 star.

📈 Quick Win Opportunity

Adding in-app troubleshooting and FAQ features (1 month effort) could immediately reduce support burden by 25%.
